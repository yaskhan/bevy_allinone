# AI System

The AI system in `bevy_allinone` focuses on behavior trees and tactical decision-making.

## Key Components

- `AiAgent`: Marker component for AI-controlled entities.
- `BehaviorTree`: Attached to agents to define their logic.

## Systems

- `ai_perception_system`: Handles sensory input (vision, sound).
- `ai_decision_system`: Processes behavior tree nodes.

## Factions and Stealth

The AI includes a faction management system and stealth mechanics, allowing for complex social and tactical interactions.
