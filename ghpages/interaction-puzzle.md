# Interactive Puzzles and Devices

Specialized systems for world puzzles and electrical devices.

## Features

- **Logic Gates**: Connections between switches, buttons, and doors.
- **Puzzle States**: Complex multi-step interaction logic.
- **Environmental Devices**: Generators, terminals, and security cameras.

## Components

- `PuzzleNode`: Part of a larger puzzle logic.
- `Device`: General interaction component for world objects.

## Systems

- `puzzle_logic_system`: Evaluates connections and state changes.
- `device_power_system`: Manages power distribution and requirements.
